## Definitions 

### 1. $\det^{-1}:$

$$
\det^{-1}(k) = \left\{M\in\mathtt{M}_2\left(\mathbb{Z}/p^{\alpha}\mathbb{Z}\right) /\quad \det M=k\right\}
$$

### 2. $\mathtt{GL}_2:$

$$
\mathtt{GL}_2(\mathbb{Z}/p^\alpha\mathbb{Z})=\bigcup_{p\ \nmid \ k} \det^{-1}(k)
$$

$$
